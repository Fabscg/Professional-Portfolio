AboutMe

People find me to be an upbeat self-motivated team player with excellent communication skills. for the past several years I have worked for customer service and hospitality, and also spearheaded my own business. Having held a lifelong affinity for coding and programming, I endeavoured to equip myself with the knowledge and skillset to pursue a career as a developer. Over the last two years, I independently dedicated myself to deepening my studies and experience in web development and acquired proficiency in the breadth of programming languages. I am a curious and driven individual, and excited for an opportunity to specialize in this dynamic industry.

Bike Haven

Bike Haven is a group project that 6 of us contribute to do a cycling/parts sales site. We use Node as the server, MySQL as the DB, Handlebars library for the templates, Bootstrap, bcript, express, Sequelize, dotenv dependencies and Heroku for the deployment. Our Challenge for this Porject was personally for me Handlebars, since I had never used it and wanted to use it in this project to challenge myself.
